Navigation

For navigation I used text links that take you each section.

Images

Images are in PNG format, and are 300px wide and 300px tall.

Videos

Videos are in mp4 format, with no autoplay, and are 320px wide and 240 px tall.

Music

Music is in both mpeg and ogg audio formats, and has no autoplay.

Report
